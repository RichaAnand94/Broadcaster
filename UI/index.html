Broadcaster - A solution for Corruption

WHAT IS BROADCASTER

Broadcaster is an online platform that allows you to create a red flag and intervene by the citizens of the country and it allows you to keep the records and track the progress of your request until it is resolved and completely done.

HOW IT WORKS

Create a request

To create a new request you are required to fill the a request form. This form will contains all of your request e.g Title, Description, Department etc. Even you can add image, video or geolocation of the requests.

Wait for approval

Once you create a request by submitting a request form, you are to wait for government authorization to intervene.

Track progress

You an easily track the progress or your request by visiting the status of your request. You can check the status of the red flag, whether your request is in active, under-investigation or rejected.
